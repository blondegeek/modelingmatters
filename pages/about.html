About

Modeling Matter(s) is a platform that includes seminars and podcasts to showcase early career researchers and the mental models they use to investigate the world around us.

Accessible, high-level recorded seminars give speakers the opportunity to share their adventures in research problem solving and informal podcasts give listeners a chance to get to know these fascinating folks pushing the boundary of human understanding. Audience participation is a crucial component of this platform so folks are encouraged to ask questions during these live seminars and podcasts and reach out to speakers and organizers.

Contact us at tess [at] modelingmatters.com.
